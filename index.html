How To Use

1

Open with care

Gently slide or lift the cardboard lid to protect the natural fiber packaging.

2

Apply directly

Glide or dab the balm directly onto desired pulse points such as the wrists, neck, behind the ears, or collarbone. Your natural body heat will soften the balm and release the fragrance.

3

Reapply as needed

Refresh throughout the day for a subtle, personal scent.

How To Dispose

1

Peel & Prep

Remove the sticker entirely (unless it's certified compostable) and wipe out any leftover waxy residue from the inside with a cloth or paper towel.

2

Shred

Tear or cut the biodegradable container into small pieces to increase the surface area, which helps it break down much faster.

3

Compost

Toss the shredded pieces into your home compost bin or bury them in soil; discard the non-biodegradable sticker in your regular trash.
